Privacy Notice

In accordance with Republic Act No. 10173 — Data Privacy Act of 2012

1. Personal Information Controller

ScamStop (developed by Team Ambergris) is the Personal Information Controller (PIC) responsible for the personal data you submit through this application. For privacy-related concerns, contact us through the About page.

2. What Data We Collect

When you submit a scam report, we collect the following personal data:

  • Victim's full name — to identify the affected party
  • City / location — to map scam incidents geographically
  • Scam message content — for AI-based threat analysis and training
  • Suspect name, contact, and alias — to assist in investigation
  • Amount lost — for statistical reporting
  • Evidence files (screenshots/PDFs) — stored securely via Uploadcare
  • Approximate location (city-level) — auto-detected via IP address or device GPS, used only to pre-fill the city field

3. Purpose and Legal Basis

Your data is collected and processed for the following purposes:

  • Detecting and flagging scam content to protect other users
  • Improving the accuracy of the ScamStop AI detection model
  • Generating anonymized statistical reports on scam trends
  • Sharing verified reports with the Philippine National Police Anti-Cybercrime Group (PNP-ACG) for law enforcement action

The legal basis for processing is your freely given, specific, and informed consent (Section 12(a), RA 10173), and the legitimate purpose of public safety and law enforcement (Section 12(f)).

4. Data Sharing

Your report data may be shared with:

  • Philippine National Police (PNP-ACG) — for investigation of verified scam cases
  • Firebase / Google Cloud — our secure cloud database provider
  • Uploadcare — for secure evidence file storage

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

5. Data Retention

Reports are retained for a maximum of 3 years from the date of submission, or until the associated case is resolved, whichever comes first. After this period, personal identifiers are anonymized or deleted.

6. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under RA 10173, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object — object to processing of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise these rights, use your Report ID to identify your submission and contact us through the About page.

7. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), access-controlled cloud storage, and anonymization of data used for AI training.

8. Complaints

If you believe your data privacy rights have been violated, you may file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) at complaints@privacy.gov.ph or visit privacy.gov.ph.

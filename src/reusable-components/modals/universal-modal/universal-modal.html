In accordance with Republic Act No. 10173 — Data Privacy Act of 2012

1. Personal Information Controller

ScamStop (developed by Team Ambergris) is the Personal Information Controller (PIC) responsible for the personal data you submit through this application.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you use ScamStop:

Report form: Victim's full name, city/location, scam message content, suspect name and contact, amount lost, and evidence files.

Real-time scanner: Text content of posts and messages visible on your screen (Facebook, Instagram, Gmail, Telegram) is sent to our AI server for scam analysis. No content is stored unless you submit a report.

Location: Approximate city-level location via IP address (extension) or device GPS (web), used only to pre-fill the city field.

3. Purpose and Legal Basis

Your data is processed to detect scam content, improve our AI model, generate anonymized statistics, and share verified reports with the PNP-ACG for law enforcement. Legal basis: your consent (Section 12(a)) and public safety (Section 12(f)) under RA 10173.

4. Data Sharing

Report data may be shared with the Philippine National Police Anti-Cybercrime Group (PNP-ACG) for verified cases, and stored via Firebase (Google Cloud) and Uploadcare. We do not sell your data.

5. Data Retention

Reports are retained for a maximum of 3 years from submission, or until the case is resolved. Personal identifiers are anonymized or deleted after this period.

6. Your Rights

Under RA 10173, you have the right to access, correct, erase, object to, and port your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. Use your Report ID to identify your submission and contact us via the About page.

7. Security

We use HTTPS encryption, access-controlled cloud storage, and data anonymization for AI training to protect your information.

8. Complaints

You may file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) at complaints@privacy.gov.ph or visit privacy.gov.ph.
